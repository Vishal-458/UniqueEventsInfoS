About Unique Events
हमारे बारे में

With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in creating magnificent Indian weddings and events that blend tradition with contemporary elegance

Creating Timeless Indian Celebrations
शाश्वत भारतीय उत्सवों का सृजन

Founded in 2008, Unique Events has become synonymous with exquisite Indian wedding planning and event management. Our expertise lies in creating celebrations that honor rich traditions while incorporating modern design elements.

2008 में स्थापित, यूनिक इवेंट्स उत्कृष्ट भारतीय शादी की योजना और कार्यक्रम प्रबंधन का पर्याय बन गया है। हमारी विशेषज्ञता समृद्ध परंपराओं का सम्मान करते हुए आधुनिक डिजाइन तत्वों को शामिल करके उत्सवों का सृजन करना है।

From grand mandap decorations to intricate mehndi ceremonies, we handle every aspect of your special day with precision and cultural sensitivity.

Our Premium Services

Luxury Tent Houses
लग्जरी टेंट हाउस

Elegant shamianas and luxury tents for outdoor weddings with traditional jhoolas, chandeliers, and climate control.

Traditional & Modern Decor
पारंपरिक और आधुनिक सजावट

From floral mandaps to contemporary stage designs, we create stunning decor blending traditional motifs with modern aesthetics.

Bridal & Groom Makeup
दुल्हन और दूल्हा मेकअप

Professional makeup artists specializing in Indian bridal looks, mehndi application, and traditional hairstyling for all ceremonies.

Authentic Indian Catering
प्रामाणिक भारतीय खानपान

Exquisite vegetarian and non-vegetarian menus featuring regional specialties, live counters, and traditional sweet displays.

Event Logistics & Transport
कार्यक्रम लॉजिस्टिक्स और परिवहन

Complete logistics management including decorated vehicles, load transportation, and setup coordination across multiple venues.

Complete Wedding Planning
